Chugga Cookies

Description

This is a cookie recipe created by famous YouTuber chuggaaconroy.

Ingredients

  • (Almost) 1 cup flour
  • (Almost) 1/2 cup of sugar
  • (Warmed) 3/4 cup butter
  • DILL PICKLE MUSTARD
  • A pinch of salt
  • A splorch of water???
  • 1 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Throw the flour and sugar into a pot
  2. Microwave the butter to "loosen" it before adding it to the pot as well
  3. Add the salt too
  4. Add the water and stir
  5. Add the eggs
  6. Stir some more until it looks dough-like
  7. Scoop the batter onto a cookie sheet
  8. Add chocolate chips
  9. Preheat oven to 375 F
  10. Bake for 20 minutes until smoking
  11. Remove from the oven and enjoy!
