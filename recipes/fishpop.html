Fish Pop

Description

This is a fish pop recipe as seen on neopets.com.

Ingredients

  • One handsaw
  • One whittling knife
  • One piece of sandpaper (rough)
  • One net
  • Four Meepits

Steps

  1. Visit your local hardware store or Pirate Plunder outlet.
  2. Purchase any tools listed above that you didn't already have in your basement.
  3. Hop on a boat to Mystery Island.
  4. Don't rock the boat.
  5. Upon arriving at Mystery Island, wander the jungles until you find a rubber tree. There's one in there somewhere.
  6. Chop down said rubber tree.
  7. Whittle the tree down to one stick, exactly 230 mm long and 5 mm in diameter.
  8. Sand said stick so as not to give the fish (or the eventual consumer of the fish) any splinters.
  9. Store the stick in a place where it won't get stolen, like your pocket or something. We'll come back to it later.
  10. Wander lost in the jungle for a few days until you come across some hungry natives.
  11. RUN!
  12. Eventually make your way to the shores, where hungry natives dare not tread.
  13. Use the net to go fishing.
  14. Use the fishing pole you just caught in the net to actually catch a fish.
  15. Retrieve the stick. Hopefully you kept it in your pocket.
  16. Put the stick in your right hand.
  17. Put the fish in your left hand.
  18. If you are left-handed, switch the stick and the fish. This step is optional.
  19. Bring your hands together.
Congratulations! Now you have a fish on a stick, otherwise known as a Fish Pop! Please remember: when making a Fish Pop, get a parent's permission first!
